About Us

Active Planet offers a variety of unique rides that cater to the spirit of every individual. With over 40 different rides, you'll find a diverse selection of attractions for children between the age of five and 15. These rides are all activity driven and hence ideal to lead into an active lifestyle. Get ready to embark on an adventure like no other at Active Planet!
At Active Planet, entertainment is an integral part of your daily life. Our mission is to provide various activities and experiences that keep you engaged, entertained, and energized every day. Step into our world of everyday entertainment, where boredom is banished, and excitement awaits at every turn. International performers would entertain you in a way that you would carry home fond memories of a time well spent.
